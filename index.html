About TriageAI

TriageAI is a free, AI-powered symptom triage tool designed for rural India. Describe your symptoms in English, Hindi, Bengali, Kannada, or Tamil and get instant guidance on when to see a doctor — along with the nearest hospitals and pharmacies.

You can also photograph a prescription, clinical note, or lab report and get a clear, plain-English breakdown of every medicine, diagnosis, and test result.

Important — Please Read

  • This is NOT a medical diagnosis
  • This does NOT replace a qualified doctor
  • This is a student research prototype — for educational purposes only
  • Your symptoms are processed by AI and are never stored or shared
  • Location data is used only to find nearby hospitals — never stored
  • The creator accepts no liability for decisions made based on this tool

🔒 Privacy

  • 🩺 Symptoms — processed by Claude AI (Anthropic). Never stored, never shared. Each session is independent.
  • 📍 Location — used only to find nearby hospitals via OpenStreetMap. Never stored or transmitted to any server.
  • 🚫 No third-party data sharing — your data is not sold or shared with advertisers or any third parties.
  • 🗑️ No data retained — all personal data is cleared when your session ends. We store nothing about you.
